World of Warships is an insanely good, highly popular game in which you get to pilot massive battleships of many different kinds and take them into destructive battles where there's no such thing as too much firepower. The game's also great because there are very few of its type, if any, out there so overall it's definitely worth giving a shot to.

New players are introduced to the game with the help of a really in-depth tutorial sequence that spans itself across the first few minutes of play time. It's a well designed tutorial and it definitely gets the job done by covering everything ranging from basic controls to some advanced mechanics and it does so within a relatively short amount of time as well.

In terms of gameplay, World of Warships is a game that's near impossible to let go of once you get into it. The gameplay is of course focused around naval warfare which might not be as fast paced as land or sky based warfare but it's definitely a lot more strategic. There are many different kinds of warships that can be utilized such as the destroyers which are nimble yet deadly or the battleships who are basically the tanks of the sea with their ridiculous cannons and borderline impenetrable armor.

The most versatile has to be the aircraft carrier as it's basically capable of unloading four different kinds of aircraft into the battlefield that can wreak havoc from above and force enemies to change their strategies.

There are two main game modes to choose from. The first is the standard team deathmatch style in which the objective is to get rid of all enemy ships as quickly as possible with minimal losses. The second is the domination mode which is basically the game's 'capture the point'. The fact that there are two different modes makes sure that players don't get bored and can alternate between them if they need a change of pace.

Strategy and teamwork are of course the most heavily emphasized on aspects when it comes to World of Warships. Proper positioning can easily net you some free damage on your opponents that they can't retaliate too at all. Maintaining good communication with teammates and going for targets together is always what you should do because going solo leads to pretty easy pickings for the opponents.

The game's visuals are absolutely delightful. The battle scenes are an incredible sight with explosions everywhere coupled with the sound of seemingly endless gunfire. The developer has put an enormous amount of attention into the detail of aircrafts and ships which is why they look surprisingly close to their real world counterparts. All things considered, the game certainly does not fail to impress when it comes to the graphics department.

There's a premium shop as well where players can purchase enhancements for their vessels, in-game currency and even some premium packages that offer various bonuses. World of Warships is the perfect blend of strategy and destruction and can be recommended to all. World of Warships Resumen
